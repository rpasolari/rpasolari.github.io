DBD Plasma Reactor

CFD analysis with thermal effects on a Dielectric Barrier Discharge plasma reactor designed for sewage sludge decontamination. Utilizing the chtMultiRegionFoam solver in OpenFOAM, the simulation aimed to measure the temperature rise in the sludge caused by plasma discharge, ensuring that decontamination was achieved due to plasma effects rather than elevated temperatures.

OpenFOAM chtMultiRegion Heat Transfer Cold Plasma Decontamination
Two-fluid model for Hydrogen plasma

Development of a 0-D steady-state model for cold Hydrogen plasma, in the pressure limits of 10-60 Torr. The code solves six balance equations for the six different species participating inm the discharge, and an energy equation for the determination of the gas' temperature. The code has been developed in Fortran 2008, and all the parameters are gained by the freeware BOLSIG+.

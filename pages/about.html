About

Hi there! I’m a Mechanical Engineer with both MEng and MSc degrees from the Mechanical Engineering and Aeronautics Department at the University of Patras in Greece. These days, I’m diving deep into my PhD at the Aerospace Faculty of TU Delft in the Netherlands. My research focuses on developing a two-dimensional hybrid Eulerian-Lagrangian solver aimed at tackling external aerodynamics challenges.
